About Me

The ability to imagine, dream, and create is innate within us all. People dream of doing something meaningful with their lives all the time, but never take the steps toward that goal. This is what drives me everyday to pursue a career where I get to create, use my imagination, and hopefully fufill a dream or two. We use things everyday, but never really stop and think that somewhere, at some point in time, someone had an idea that eventually transformed our way of life. That is the adventure this industry provides on a daily basis. It also lends itself to allow us to have some fun along the way.

I have been a 3D artist for the past five years and have worked with some high profile brands such as Reebok, North Face, Timberland, and Keen. My goal with any project has always been to make the product "work", whether through established techniques or through innovative ones. I made a realization that programming was a necessary skill so I decided to make the cross over to web programming in order to further my skills in the the world of 3D and hopefully immerse myself in the world of virtual reality. Programming is the tool to further my own and others ideas into making them a reality.
